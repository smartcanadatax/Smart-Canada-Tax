RRSP vs TFSA Comparison

TFSA Annual Limit (2025)$7,000
Cumulative TFSA Room (since 2009)$102,000
RRSP: Pre-tax contribution. Tax-deferred growth. Taxable on withdrawal. Best if you're in a high bracket now and expect lower income in retirement.

TFSA: After-tax contribution. Tax-free growth. Tax-free withdrawal. Best for flexibility — no income impact on OAS/GIS/EI clawbacks.
