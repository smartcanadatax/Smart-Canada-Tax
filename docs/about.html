About Us

Built for Canadians,

Smart Canada Tax was created to make Canadian tax calculations accessible, fast, and free — without needing an accountant for every question.

Our Mission

Tax in Canada is complicated. Thirteen provinces and territories, two official languages, personal and corporate rules that change every year — most Canadians don't have a CPA on speed dial. Smart Canada Tax exists to close that gap: give every Canadian a fast, accurate way to estimate their tax situation and make smarter financial decisions.

What We Built

Smart Canada Tax is a free iOS app covering the most common Canadian tax scenarios — personal income tax, corporate tax, GST/HST, RRSP, rental income, self-employment, benefits, and more. Every calculator uses the current 2025 CRA rates for all 13 provinces and territories.

The app also includes an AI Tax Assistant powered by a large language model trained to understand Canadian tax law — T1 personal returns, T2 corporate returns, CCA classes, GST/HST thresholds, and more. Ask a question in plain English, get a plain English answer.

For questions that go beyond what an app can answer, we offer live 30-minute sessions with a qualified Canadian tax professional via Google Meet — at a fraction of what an accountant charges per hour.
