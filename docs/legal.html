Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026  ·  1000936219 Ontario Inc.

Smart Canada Tax is built with privacy as a core principle. All tax calculations happen entirely on your device — no financial data is ever sent to our servers.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Smart Canada Tax does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Income figures, tax estimates, or any financial data entered into calculators
  • Your Social Insurance Number (SIN), date of birth, or government identifiers
  • Your name, address, or personal contact information (except contact inquiries you voluntarily submit)
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Device identifiers linked to your financial inputs
  • Behavioural analytics or usage tracking tied to any personal identifier

All calculator inputs are processed entirely on your device and are never sent anywhere.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store booked session details and app preferences locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data remains solely on your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and is not accessible to us or any third party. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

3. Contact Inquiries

If you submit a message through the Services or Contact section of the app or website, your name, email address, and message are transmitted to admin@smartcanadatax.help. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not store it in any database, share it with third parties, or use it for marketing.

4. In-App Purchases & Advisory Sessions

Any in-app purchases or session bookings are processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment card details or billing information. Session bookings may be coordinated through Calendly, subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

This App does not embed any advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or user-tracking tools. We do not serve advertisements.

  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy
  • Calendly — session booking coordination, subject to Calendly's Privacy Policy

6. AI Tax Guidance

If you use the Tax AI feature, your typed queries are sent to our AI provider over an encrypted HTTPS connection to generate a response. We do not log or store these conversations on our servers. Financial data entered into calculators is never sent to the AI — only the text you type in the AI chat.

7. Apple App Store & iOS

Apple may collect certain usage and crash data as part of standard iOS operation, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not control or have access to data collected by Apple.

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

9. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your financial data on our servers, the risk of a server-side data breach exposing your financial information does not apply. Data stored on your device is protected by Apple's built-in device encryption.

10. PIPEDA / GDPR / CCPA

Because we collect no personal financial data and store nothing on our servers, data protection obligations relating to financial data processing do not apply to our core operations. We do not sell personal information. All data remains under your sole control on your device.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected with an updated date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
