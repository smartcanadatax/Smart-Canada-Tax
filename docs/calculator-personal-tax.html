About This Calculator

Uses 2025 CRA rates: federal brackets, all 10 provinces and 3 territories, Canada Pension Plan (CPP1 + CPP2), Employment Insurance (EI), Quebec QPP/QPIP, Quebec abatement, Ontario Health Premium, Ontario surtax, BPA tapering for high earners, Canada Employment Amount, and Line 22215 enhanced CPP deduction. Capital gains use the 50% inclusion rate. Results match the Smart Canada Tax mobile app.

⚠️ This calculator provides estimates only. For personalized advice, consult a licensed Canadian tax professional.
