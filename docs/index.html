📱 About the App

Canadian Tax Calculator is an iOS app built to help Canadians estimate their tax obligations quickly and accurately. Whether you're an individual filer, a freelancer, or a small business owner, the app covers the most common Canadian federal and provincial tax scenarios.

The app is for estimation and planning purposes only and does not file tax returns or provide professional accounting advice.
