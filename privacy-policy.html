SmartCanadaTax — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

SmartCanadaTax is designed with your privacy as a core principle. This app does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal financial information you enter into the calculators.

1. Overview

SmartCanadaTax ("the App") is an iOS application for Canadian tax estimation. This policy explains exactly what data is and is not collected when you use the App.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store on our servers, or transmit:

  • Income figures, tax estimates, or any financial data entered into calculators
  • Your Social Insurance Number (SIN), date of birth, or government identifiers
  • Your name, address, or personal contact information (except as described below)
  • Precise or approximate location data
  • Device identifiers linked to your financial inputs
  • Behavioural analytics tied to any personal identifier

All calculator inputs are processed entirely on your device and are never sent anywhere.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store session bookings and user preferences locally on your device using Apple's secure on-device storage (UserDefaults). This data remains solely on your device, is never transmitted to our servers, and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Contact Form & Inquiry Data

If you submit a contact inquiry through the Services tab, your name, email address, and message are transmitted directly to smartcanadatax@gmail.com via your device's Mail app. This information is used solely to respond to your request. We do not store this data in any database, share it with third parties, or use it for marketing without your consent.

5. Third-Party Services

This App integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Apple In-App Purchase (StoreKit) — used for payment processing of advisory sessions. Payments are handled entirely by Apple and subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment card details.
  • Calendly — used for booking session time slots. When you book through Calendly, Calendly's Privacy Policy and Terms apply.

This App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or user-tracking SDKs. We do not serve advertisements of any kind.

6. Apple App Store & iOS

Apple may collect certain usage and crash data as part of standard iOS app operation, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. We do not control or have access to data collected by Apple.

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store your financial data on our servers, the risk of a data breach exposing your financial information is minimized. Any data stored on-device is protected by your device's built-in security and encryption.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not maintain a database of your personal information, there is no profile to access, correct, or delete on our end. For any inquiry-related data sent to our email, you may contact us at smartcanadatax@gmail.com to request deletion.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date in the App. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact us at smartcanadatax@gmail.com.
